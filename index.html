Nicolas Le Guillarme

Hi, I'm Nicolas Le Guillarme, an independent research engineer specializing in ecoinformatics and artificial intelligence applied to ecology and biodiversity sciences.

My mission is to make cutting-edge artificial intelligence techniques available to researchers to help them improve our understanding of biodiversity.

Services

I offer a range of consulting and project development services for researchers, organizations, and companies working in ecology and biodiversity sciences.

  • AI & Machine Learning in Ecology - Developing algorithms for ecological data analysis, pattern recognition, and prediction.
  • Ecoinformatics Solutions - Developing computational tools and methods to access, integrate and manage ecological information.
  • Ecological Knowledge Engineering - Providing tailor-made AI solutions to make better use of existing ecological knowledge, from knowledge acquisition to decision-making.
  • Custom AI Tools - Building custom AI tools and applications to support research in ecology and biodiversity.

Research Areas

My research spans a variety of areas at the intersection of ecology, biodiversity, and artificial intelligence:

  • AI for Biodiversity Monitoring - Developing automated systems for monitoring species and ecosystems using AI.
  • Climate Change Modeling - Using machine learning to predict the impacts of climate change on ecosystems and biodiversity.
  • Ecological Network Analysis - Applying network theory and AI to study ecological relationships and community dynamics.
  • Data Integration in Ecoinformatics - Integrating environmental and biodiversity data to create holistic models of ecological systems.
